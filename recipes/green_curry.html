JACOB'S AWESOME GREEN CURRY

This is my awesome green curry. I learnt to make this curry when my 2nd child had just been born. I was in the delivery room and my Uncle ran in.

"Jacob, Come Quick!" He shouted, "We need an awesome curry stat."

My wife, mid labour, turned to me and said "Go! You're a chef first and a husband second!" and I ran out of the room to make this amazing curry.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need:
  • Some Chicken breast, neatly diced
  • Some Curry Paste, Green
  • Faith
  • Trust
  • Pixie Dust

Steps

Failure to follow these steps will result in potentially catastrophic events
  1. Start by building a stove, be careful not to do this without a proper plumbing and gas certification.
  2. Once finished, Cook! It's that easy.
